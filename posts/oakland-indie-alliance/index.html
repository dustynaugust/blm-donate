Oakland Indie Alliance (OIA)

Donate Here

Founded in 2014, the Oakland Indie Alliance (OIA) is a community of more than 300 small-scale entrepreneurs who have united under a common goal of preserving Oakland’s variety, energy, and social fabric. Through sharing experiences, connections, and advocating for Oakland’s independent economy, OIA allows small businesses to thrive.
