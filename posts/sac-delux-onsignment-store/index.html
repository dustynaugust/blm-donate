SacDelux Consignment Store

Donate Here

SacDelux is a luxury consignment business, built, owned and operated by Shanice Gaither and Nikki Hightower.

Since 2014, these ladies have worked to establish their business & brand not only for means to support theirselves, but also the profitability of individuals.

As a consignment store, a large majority of their inventory consists of gently used luxury items provided by clients looking for a return on their luxury investments.

Although insurance and theft protection measures were take to secure their clients belongings, the fight to reclaim the losses they have experienced will be ongoing and not guaranteed.

While the cost of their inventory and damages are astronomical every dollar will count toward reopening this small business and ensuring that all clients receive compensation for trusting SacDelux as their preferred luxury consignment store.
