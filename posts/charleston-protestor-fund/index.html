Charleston, SC Protestor Fund

The Charleston, SC Protestor Fund is a general community fund to help any Black Lives Matter protestors in the Charleston, SC area receive bail funds should they need it. We are in the early stages so please stayed tuned for more info. This is not officially affiliated with any organization or organizer, this is created by allies wanting to help create positive change.
