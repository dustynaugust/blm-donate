Montgomery Bail Out

Donate Here

We are raising money to provide support and post bail for people in the Montgomery County Jail. Our friends, loved ones, and comrades in Montgomery's crowded, unsanitary jails are especially endangered by the ongoing spread of COVID-19. When our government leaders fail to take appropriate action to keep us safe, the community must step up. That is why we are inviting you to join us in launching the Montgomery Bail Out.
