The Mississippi Bail Fund Collective

The Mississippi Bail Fund Collective was created to put an end to the injustice caused by the bail system. We bail poor people out of jail! And we offer compassionate help to reverse the inevitable damage that they and their families face after an unjust imprisonment. Your donation will ensure everyday Mississippians have the same chance to receive justice as those that have money. Every dollar makes a difference!
